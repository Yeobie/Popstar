Discography

Slide to discover all of their albums!

2 Cool 4 Skool

June 12th, 2013

Their debut single album after almost 3 years of training. The title track is 'No More Dream', a song that invites
today's youth to break out of society’s restrictions and to find their own dream.

O!RUL8,2?

September 11th, 2013

Their first mini-album. The title track is 'N.O', a message to kids who leave a life aimed to only satisfy adults' needs and wishes, inviting them to find their true happiness before it's too late.

Skool Luv Affair

February 12th, 2014

Their second mini-album. It has a thematic shift from dream and happiness to school age and youthful love.

Dark&Wild

August 20th, 2014

Their first studio album. It explores the hip hop direction BTS is taking,
and it's a continuation of the theme of the previous album.

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Pt. 1

April 29th, 2015

Their third mini-album. It explores the hardships of young adults, and the themes of pain, depression, and anger. Starting from the title track, 'I Need U', they share stories where each person needs someone to be with.

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Pt. 2

November 30th, 2015

Their fourth mini-album. The title track, 'RUN', has a more adventurous and daring feel. It explores the concept of rebelling against the adult world, challenging it to tame them.

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever

May 2nd, 2016

Their first compilation album, including tracks from the previous two albums and three new songs. The title track, 'Fire', is one of their most famous songs and topped the Billboard World Digital Songs chart.

Wings

October 10th, 2016

Their second studio album, with 'Blood, Sweat and Tears' as title track.. Influenced by Hermann Hesse's coming of age novel, Demian, this album deals with temptation and growth. It sold more than 1 million copies.

You Never Walk Alone

February 17th, 2017

A repackaging of Wings, with three additional songs. The title track, 'Spring Day', was named by Rolling Stone one of the greatest boy band songs of all time. It was an instant success on YouTube, garnering over 10 million views in 24 hours, becoming the fastest K-pop video to do so at the time.

Love Yourself: Her

September 18th, 2017

Their fifth mini-album, which debuted at #7 on Billboard 200. The lead single, DNA, became the first K-pop group music video to reach over 20 million views in 24 hours.

Love Yourself: Tear

May 18th, 2018

Their third studio album. It debuted at #1 on Billboard 200 and it explores themes relating to the pains and sorrows of separation. The title track is 'Fake Love'. The clip achieved 35.9 million views on the platform on its first day. It also became the third most watched music video within the first 24 hours in 2018.

Love Yourself: Answer

August 24th, 2018

Their second compilation album and second best-selling album of 2018 in the world. The title track is 'IDOL', and it became the most watched music video within the first 24 hours on the platform.

Map of the Soul: Persona

April 12th, 2019

Their sixth mini album and third best-selling album of 2019 in the world, with 'Boy with Luv' as title track, describing how true strength and love come from finding joy in the smallest things in life. It was also the fastest video to reach 100 million views on YouTube, doing so in approximately one day and 10 hours.

Map of the Soul: 7

February 21st, 2020

Their fourth studio album and best-selling album of 2020 in the world. The title track is 'ON': the seventh most-watched online video in the first 24 hours, and the fifth most-watched YouTube video at the time.

Be

November 20th, 2020

Their first self-directed and seventh mini album. Created in response to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, it was inspired by BTS' perspective of life during the pandemic. The title track, 'Dynamite', their first song fully recorded in English, intended to soothe listeners during the pandemic.

Proof

June 10th, 2022

Their first anthology album. It comprises several of the band's singles; a selection of discography "favorites" chosen by the band members; and various demos and previously unreleased tracks. The title track is 'Yet to Come': lyrically, the group reflects on their career of the previous nine years and looks forward to the future.

Some of their (many) records

BTS has set and broken a huge number of world records. Some of them are listed below, but you can find a complete list here.

Longest-charting K-pop album on Billboard World Albums Chart

With their album "Wings" and its repackaging "You Never Walk Alone", 37 weeks.

Highest-charting K-pop group album of all time

No.1 with their album
"Love Yourself: Tear".

First Korean song on the No. 1 Billboard Hot 100 chart

With their song "Life Goes On", on December 5th 2020.

Most liked song in 24 hours

2 million of likes on "MIC Drop (Steve Aoki Remix), on November 25th, 2017.

Fastest K-pop group music video to reach 100 million views

"Boy With Luv (feat. Halsey)", on April 14th 2019. It set this record in
1 day, 13 hours, and 38 minutes.

Most viewers for a music concert live stream

756,000 people watching
BANG BANG CON, their first online concert on June 14th 2020, during the
COVID-19 pandemic.

Fastest music video to reach 10 million views

Their music video "ON", in just
1 hour and 5 minutes, on February 28th, 2020.

Most viewed YouTube music video in 24 hours

With their song "Butter", 108.2 million views. They broke their own record previously set with "Dynamite" (101.1 million views).
