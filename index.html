Get to know them!

"Hello! We are BTS!"

Bangtan Sonyeondan (방탄소년단 - Bulletproof Boys), or BTS, are a South-Korean group that was formed in the early 2010s and debuted on June 13th, 2013. With nine studio albums, seven complilation albums and six extended plays, the record-breaking group is arguably the biggest boy idol group in the world, with many chart-topping hits.
BTS has strongly influenced the world with its heartfelt messages of consolation and hope for youth, and spoke about major subjects such as self-harm, academic stress, inferiority complex, bullying, depression, elitism, and mental health. They are the first Asian group to win Best Artist at the American Music Awards and first all Korean band to perform at the Grammy Awards and to receive a nomination. They were the first Korean group to win a Billboard Music Award, and in 2021 they collaborated with Coldplay for the song "My Universe".
Data suggests that the band brings over $3.9 billion to the South Korean economy. The Hyundai Research institute found that about 800,000 visitors visit South Korea because of BTS every year.

ARMY, you are our wings.
Because of you all, we can fly.” BTS

From being underdogs in the music industry (their production house, Big Hit Entertainment, had initially very little money) to becoming a record-breaking group with billions of streams, BTS also humbly credits their success to their fandom, the ARMY.
The ARMY members are young, liberal, international, influential, and masters of social media. They stand apart from other fandoms through the ways they have mobilized with an unrivaled level of organization and dedication, driven by a desire to see the seven members of BTS leave their mark in territories previously uncharted by any other pop act from South Korea. BTS fans are also known to have contributed to hundreds of charitable causes (saving more than 5000 lives, raising 1.5M for BTS UNICEF campaign, planting 50.000 trees etc.). BTS and Big Hit Entertainment donated US$1 million to Black Lives Matter, announcing they stand together against racial discrimination. The ARMY matched the donation in 24 hours.
Based on the 2022 ARMY Census, there are currently more than 500,000 ARMY in the world. You can read all the results of the census here.

The members

Born September 12th, 1994, he is the leader and first member of BTS. He's the one in the group who speaks English most fluently (and he says he learned it by watching Friends) and acts as the spokesperson of BTS. He's a skilled rapper (RM stands for Rap Monster) and part of the group's rapline made up by the three rappers: him, Suga, and
J-Hope. Together with them, he's the writer of many of the group's songs. He recently produced his first studio album, "Indigo", with 'Wild Flower' as title track.

Listen to Wild Flower

Born December 4th, 1992, he's the oldest member of the group and part of the vocal line, made up by the four vocals: him, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Jin's laughter is contagious and he's the funniest member of the group: he usually makes dad jokes and he's self-proclaimed "World Wide Handsome", a recurrent joke he says to make the members and ARMY smile. He also loves to cook for the other members. Jin recently released his single, "The Astronaut", a song that was created for him by none other than... Coldplay!

Listen to The Astronaut

Born March 9th, 1993, Yoongi was the second member who entered in the group after RM. He's an amazing rapper, song-writer and composer, and also part of the group's rapline. Like Jin, he likes to cook for the other members. He's serious-looking and may also seem reserved and shy, but when he's with his group he's very laidback and loves to have fun with them. He's also a soloist: with the pseudonym of Agust D, he's published two mixtapes. Besides, he wrote Psy's latest success, 'That That', and he performed in it too.

Listen to That That

Born February 18th, 1994, he's the group main dancer and a rapper: he was the third member to enter the group after RM and Suga. He introduces himself by saying: "I'm your hope, you're my hope, I'm J-Hope!". Faithfully to his name, he acts as the positive energy glue for the team with his cheerful personality; he loves goofing around and making weird sounds; but he's also the best dancer and he can be very strict when it comes to choreographies: the other members are always nervous to make mistakes in front of him! In 2022 he published his first solo album: "Jack in The Box", with 'More' as title track.

Listen to More

Born October 13th, 1995, Jimin was the last member to enter the group in 2012, one year before their debut. He's the most skilled dancer alongside J-Hope, and his classic dance moves are outstanding - he also does the solo dance breaks. He loves receiving attention from fans and he's very affectionate to them. He's in the vocal line and has a clear, smooth, falsetto voice that often sounds feminine but can also turn masculine when he growls. His solo songs really deliver all the potential of his voice - for example, the song 'Serendipity'.

Listen to Serendipity

Born December 30th, 1995, Taehyung entered the group at the end of 2011 and he's one of the members of the vocal line. BTS' and ARMY's official color is purple and it's thanks to him: at the end of a concert, he said "I purple you. Do you know what purple means? Purple is the last color of the rainbow so it means I will trust and love you for a long time." He's kind, generous, playful with the members and very affectionate to them - he's prone to skinship and hugs them a lot. He loves jazz music, likes to dress elegantly or in a vintage style and has a deep, soothing, soulful baritone voice that you can enjoy in songs such as 'Singularity'.

Listen to Singularity

Born September 1st, 1997, he's the youngest member ('maknae') of the group, and he joined them (choosing Big Hit over 7 other agencies) after listening to RM's rap and falling in love with it. He's in the vocal line. He has a kind heart, deeply loves his fans, and everyone calls him 'Golden Maknae' because he's multi-talented: he has an incredible voice but he's also a great dancer, has strong physical abilities and he's also good at painting. He gets the majorities of the solos and is the front lead dancer. One of his most famous solos is 'Euphoria', where you can really get a clear idea of the high notes he can reach with his voice.

Listen to Euphoria

Discography

Slide to discover all of their albums!

2 Cool 4 Skool

June 12th, 2013

Their debut single album after almost 3 years of training. The title track is 'No More Dream', a song that invites
today's youth to break out of society’s restrictions and to find their own dream.

O!RUL8,2?

September 11th, 2013

Their first mini-album. The title track is 'N.O', a message to kids who leave a life aimed to only satisfy adults' needs and wishes, inviting them to find their true happiness before it's too late.

Skool Luv Affair

February 12th, 2014

Their second mini-album. It has a thematic shift from dream and happiness to school age and youthful love.

Dark&Wild

August 20th, 2014

Their first studio album. It explores the hip hop direction BTS is taking,
and it's a continuation of the theme of the previous album.

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Pt. 1

April 29th, 2015

Their third mini-album. It explores the hardships of young adults, and the themes of pain, depression, and anger. Starting from the title track, 'I Need U', they share stories where each person needs someone to be with.

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Pt. 2

November 30th, 2015

Their fourth mini-album. The title track, 'RUN', has a more adventurous and daring feel. It explores the concept of rebelling against the adult world, challenging it to tame them.

The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever

May 2nd, 2016

Their first compilation album, including tracks from the previous two albums and three new songs. The title track, 'Fire', is one of their most famous songs and topped the Billboard World Digital Songs chart.

Wings

October 10th, 2016

Their second studio album, with 'Blood, Sweat and Tears' as title track. Influenced by Hermann Hesse's coming of age novel, Demian, this album deals with temptation and growth. It sold more than 1 million copies.

You Never Walk Alone

February 17th, 2017

A repackaging of Wings, with three additional songs. The title track, 'Spring Day', was named by Rolling Stone one of the greatest boy band songs of all time. It was an instant success on YouTube, garnering over 10 million views in 24 hours, becoming the fastest K-pop video to do so at the time.

Love Yourself: Her

September 18th, 2017

Their fifth mini-album, which debuted at #7 on Billboard 200. The lead single, DNA, became the first K-pop group music video to reach over 20 million views in 24 hours.

Love Yourself: Tear

May 18th, 2018

Their third studio album. It debuted at #1 on Billboard 200 and it explores themes relating to the pains and sorrows of separation. The title track is 'Fake Love'. The clip achieved 35.9 million views on the platform on its first day. It also became the third most watched music video within the first 24 hours in 2018.

Love Yourself: Answer

August 24th, 2018

Their second compilation album and second best-selling album of 2018 in the world. The title track is 'IDOL', and it became the most watched music video within the first 24 hours on the platform.

Map of the Soul: Persona

April 12th, 2019

Their sixth mini album and third best-selling album of 2019 in the world, with 'Boy with Luv' as title track, describing how true strength and love come from finding joy in the smallest things in life. It was also the fastest video to reach 100 million views on YouTube, doing so in approximately one day and 10 hours.

Map of the Soul: 7

February 21st, 2020

Their fourth studio album and best-selling album of 2020 in the world. The title track is 'ON': the seventh most-watched online video in the first 24 hours, and the fifth most-watched YouTube video at the time.

Be

November 20th, 2020

Their first self-directed and seventh mini album. Created in response to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, it was inspired by BTS' perspective of life during the pandemic. The title track, 'Dynamite', their first song fully recorded in English, intended to soothe listeners during the pandemic.

Proof

June 10th, 2022

Their first anthology album. It comprises several of the band's singles; a selection of discography "favorites" chosen by the band members; and various demos and previously unreleased tracks. The title track is 'Yet to Come': lyrically, the group reflects on their career of the previous nine years and looks forward to the future.
